Debrief

When you played the game, you were randomly assigned to one of two groups to a play a version of Space Oddity with or without self-efficacy inducing features.

Our goals are:

  • To determine if self-efficacy supporting features increase players' self-efficacy in Space Oddity, in video games, and in life.
  • To determine if self-efficacy supporting features affect player experience in regards to enjoyment and domination over the game.
